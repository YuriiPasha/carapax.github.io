NEXT-GENERATION BALLISTIC PROTECTION ENABLED BY ADVANCED WEAVING TECHNOLOGY

We develop next-generation ballistic protection for the modern battlefield. Present and future military conflicts are driving demand for a new generation of ballistic protection. Our proprietary weaving technology enables the production of advanced multilayer ballistic 3D textiles.

The problems with current bulletproof vests:

Excessive weight
Movements restriction
Small protection area
Cannot be repaired

Our solution,

consists of specially shaped tiles from extremely light composite material, attached to a flexible fabric layer. These tiles interlock with no gaps, even when the armor is bent

and threaded fasteners on each segment allows for multiple connection and disconnection to\from basic flexible layer:

Yes, it’s that simple. But such simple invention leads to groundbreaking

UNIQUE ADVANTAGES:

Hard tiles (segments) from proprietary ultra-light composite material can effectively stop the bullet, but the protection remains comfortable to wear due to flexibility of basic fabric layer.

Due to threaded fasteners on each segment, our armor is repairable (damaged tiles could be replaced) and modular (user can choose protection level of each individual tile).

Vests could be female-fit due to flexibility and anatomical shape.
